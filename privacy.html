Privacy Policy

QR Code Generator is a Chrome extension designed with privacy as a core principle.

What data do we collect?

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or user content. All data used by this extension — including text, links, and generated QR codes — remains strictly local to your browser and device.

Use of Storage

The extension uses chrome.storage.local solely to save your recent QR code history. This data is not shared with anyone and never leaves your browser.

Permissions

We only request the minimum permissions required to operate (such as storage and contextMenus). These are used to store data locally and offer right-click QR code generation for selected text or links.

No tracking or analytics

This extension does not use cookies, analytics, tracking scripts, advertising, or any third-party services.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, feel free to contact us at your-email@example.com.
